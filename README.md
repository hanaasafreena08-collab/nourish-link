# nourish-link
food donation app

**overview:**

Our Food Donation App is a platform that connects restaurants with surplus food to NGOs and communities in need, helping reduce food waste while addressing hunger. Restaurants and NGOs can easily register on the app, where restaurants post available surplus food and NGOs post their requirements.

The platform includes essential features like contact information sharing and live map locations to enable smooth coordination between donors and receivers. An AI-powered chatbot assistant is integrated to handle user queries and guide users through the process.

To ensure quick action, the app provides instant alert notifications for urgent donations, allowing NGOs to accept or decline requests in real time. Once accepted, the app offers map-based pickup and drop navigation, similar to ride-booking services, making the logistics seamless.

To encourage participation, the app features a leaderboard for restaurants that consistently donate, along with a rating system where NGOs can review food quality. It also includes a short cancellation window (5–10 minutes) to handle unexpected situations. Additionally, the platform helps in the promotion of socially responsible restaurants, giving them visibility for their contributions.

Overall, the app creates an efficient, transparent, and rewarding ecosystem for food donation, ensuring surplus food reaches those who need it most.
